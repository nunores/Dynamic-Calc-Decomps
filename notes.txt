Console Commands

pressing tab auto completes with either highlighted AI move or AI move that does most dmg to current in pokemon
also can shorten move to first letter of each word "Dark Pulse" => "dp"
!baits move1 !after move2 - same as above but after taking max damage from move2


!doesN - all rolls do at least N% damage
!doesNWithBoostN -  all rolls do at least N damage with N% dmg boost 

!1v1sc - wins 1v1 assuming ai crits
!1v1s - wins 1v1 assuming no crits
!1v1safterc move - wins1v1 after switching in on move crit
!1v1safter move -  wins1v1 after switching in on move

!immune - mons that have immunities to one or more ai moves
!immune move - mons that are immune to move

!allmoves boost - prints all damage ranges for moves in learnset and obtained tms



Very Advanced Commands




!ppstallpairs - pairs of mons that are immune to a move and bait another move that the other is immune to, include regenerators that take less than 33%



!stopBadOdds - lists moves from pokemon's learnset that let player mon win the 1v1 if AI mon switches in on them, includes TMs and learnset up to level

!plan -  Either find a mon that can switch in on any move and still win the 1v1 OR
		 Find a mon that can switch in on any move and then baits a move onto another mon that will win the 1v1 after taking the move

!simpleplan - Finds a mon that can win the 1v1 after switching in on lowest damaging move, then finds all the mons that bait that move

!plan move - Either finds a mon that can tank the specified move and win the 1v1 or finds a mon that tanks the specified move and then baits a move that 			   onto something else that would then win the 1v1
