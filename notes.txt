Console Commands


!baits move1 !after move2 - same as above but after taking max damage from move2 and max damage crit

!moves boost - prints all damage ranges for moves in learnset and obtained tms



Advanced Commands

!ppstall - pairs of mons that are immune to a move and bait another move that the other is immune to, include regenerators that take less than 33%

!stopBadOdds - lists moves from pokemon's learnset that let player mon win the 1v1 if AI mon switches in on them, includes TMs and learnset up to level

!plan -  Either find a mon that can switch in on any move and still win the 1v1 OR
		 Find a mon that can switch in on any move and then baits a move onto another mon that will win the 1v1 after taking the move

!simpleplan - Finds a mon that can win the 1v1 after switching in on lowest damaging move, then finds all the mons that bait that move

!plan move - Either finds a mon that can tank the specified move and win the 1v1 or finds a mon that tanks the specified move and then baits a move that 			   onto something else that would then win the 1v1
